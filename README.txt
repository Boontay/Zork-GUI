When the game is first booted up, the user is presented with their health and stamina levels, alongside the possible directions to go
to enter the next room.

To view the character stats and inventory, the user can click on "View Character Info". To view
information relating to how to play the game, the user can click on "Help".

Clicking on "North", "South", "West", and "East" will make the player move into the room in the respective direction that is clicked,
provided there is a room in that direction.

By clicking on "Teleport", the user will be teleported to a random room in the game.

Clicking on "View Map" will display the map of rooms in the game.

Clicking on "View Room Items" will list the items found in the current room, and will allow them to be picked up and stored in the
chracter's inventory. These items can be transfered by double-clicking on the menu items. Likewise, the "View Character Items" button lists the items found in the player's inventory.

The game's end states are: the losing state is when the total weight of the items being carried by the player exceeds 4g, while the winning
state is when the player enters the room "j".
